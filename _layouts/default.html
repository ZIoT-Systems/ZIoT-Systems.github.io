About ZIoT

ZIot Systems builds durable sensor devices and a secure communications dashboard for industrial asset monitoring. Attach our sensors to machines to measure vibration, force, and other critical signals—each device can monitor up to eight points. Customize triggers, schedule readings, or read on-demand from a local dashboard on your network.

  • Quick install: Device up and running in minutes.
  • Flexible integration: Works alongside PLCs, CNC, conveyors, and custom assemblies.
  • Local-first: Keep data on-premises with optional encrypted cloud forwarding.
