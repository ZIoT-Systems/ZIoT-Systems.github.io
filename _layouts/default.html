About ZIoT

ZIoT Systems offers durable sensors that attach securely to equipment, providing monitoring of vibration, force, and other parameters.

Our devices connect via Wi-Fi, sending data to a secure, user-friendly dashboard. Each device can monitor up to eight points with customizable triggers and periodic readings.
